  • Music for your Earholes

    Music is one of my favorite things in the world. I've had a large history of listening to different genres and artists. I think I listen to a lot of music because I'm jealous of the musician's skills.

    Here are some of my favorite albums right now. Seriously, ya'll need to check these out!

    Blackmill - Miracle

    A beautiful piece of work.

    This is the music you want to listen too when you have nothing else to do. Go ahead, just focus on this music and find all of the layers and nuances.

    Above & Beyond - Group Therapy

    Above & Beyond has one of my favorite podcasts right now. They are considered in the top 5 most influential Trance acts in the world right now.

    What's unique about ABGT is a blend of electronic sounds and traditional studio instruments.

    The Weeknd - Trilogy

    One of the most unique R&B artists in recent years. The Weeknd will take your earholes on a journey not experienced by most. Heavy drugs, intense loves, and solemn loneliness.

    This music makes you feel something that you've never felt before.

    Daft Punk - Random Access Memories

    ROBOTS. I mean, come on! How can it get any better?!

  • Food for Thought

    I'm addicted to food shows.

    No Reservations with Anthony Bourdain, Kitchen Nightmares (the British version) with Gordon Ramsay, and Bizzare Foods with Andrew Zimmern are incredibly entertaining programs that at the same time make my mouth water like Niagra Falls.

    Honestly, how can people watch these shows and NOT be extremely jealous of these individuals?! They've got it made!

    I've learned a lot from these shows about the kitchen. Cooking is something I've always wanted to learn about and I always make an effort to learn new things.

    And the best part about learning how to cook is the delicious by-products. Obviously.
